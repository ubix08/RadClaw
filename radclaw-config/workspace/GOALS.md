# GOALS.md — Rad Business Mission & Objectives

---

## NORTH STAR MISSION

> Build a profitable, self-sustaining solo digital product business for Rachid.
> Zero subscriptions. Zero customer support. Zero custom work.
> Steady, growing passive income from info-products on Gumroad and Etsy.

---

## STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES

### Objective 1: Establish Product-Market Fit
Find and validate at least **3 product formats** that sell consistently in our target niches before expanding further.

Success signal: 3 products with >5 sales each in first 60 days.

### Objective 2: Build a Reliable Pipeline Cadence
Launch **1 new product every 3–4 weeks** on average. Speed is the competitive advantage of a solo operator.

Success signal: Pipeline always has ≥1 product in BUILDING or READY status.

### Objective 3: Achieve Revenue Baseline
Reach **$500/month recurring** from existing products before aggressively scaling new ones.

Success signal: Monthly Gumroad/Etsy revenue ≥ $500 for 2 consecutive months.

### Objective 4: Build a Scalable Format Library
Identify the **top 3 formats** that maximize margin/effort ratio for Rachid's skills. Double down on those. Drop formats that underperform.

Success signal: 80% of new products use our top 3 confirmed formats.

### Objective 5: Establish Brand Presence
Build recognizable presence in at least **1 niche** where Rachid's store becomes a known resource.

Success signal: Repeat buyers. Etsy/Gumroad followers. External traffic.

---

## CONSTRAINTS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

These are not preferences. They are hard rules that define the business model:

1. **No SaaS or subscriptions** — one-time payment products only
2. **No custom client work** — products are scalable, not bespoke
3. **No customer support overhead** — products must be self-explanatory with minimal support questions
4. **No inventory, shipping, or physical products**
5. **No upfront ad spend** (until revenue baseline is proven)
6. **No products that require ongoing maintenance** (static deliverables only, or clearly versioned)

## SUCCESS DEFINITION

We are succeeding when:
- Rachid checks Gumroad/Etsy revenue without anxiety — it's predictable and growing
- The pipeline always has a product in motion
- Market discoveries drive product decisions, not guesswork
- Post-mortems make us smarter, not just sadder
- Rad proactively identifies opportunities Rachid hasn't thought of yet

We are failing when:
- Products are launched without validation
- Products sit LIVE with no observation
- The pipeline is empty or stalled
- We repeat the same mistakes without writing post-mortems
- Rachid has to chase Rad for updates instead of the other way around
